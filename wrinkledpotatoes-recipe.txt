Ingredients:
 - 1/4 Kg of unskinned potatoes 
 - Salt, lots of salt
 - 2 litres of water

Directions:
1. Pour the water in a pot.
2. Put one potatoe inside the water, it should sink.
3. Gradually add salt to the water, and stop when the potatoe float.
4. Add the rest of the potatoes (note that the potatoes must be unskinned, if not they will never wrikle).
5. Cook until the core of the potatoes is soft, about 20 mins.
